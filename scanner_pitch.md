# SaathiCare: The Good Samaritan Protocol (Pitch)

*Use this script/outline when demoing or pitching the QR Scanner feature to judges.*

---

## 📸 The Problem
“One of the most terrifying realities for families living with Alzheimer’s is the risk of elopement—wandering off and getting lost. Traditional GPS trackers get left at home, and medical alert bracelets only offer a phone number, leaving the patient confused and whoever finds them completely unequipped to handle a dementia crisis.”

## 💡 The Solution (Live Demo Flow)
“We built the **Good Samaritan Protocol** into SaathiCare. Every patient receives a simple, static QR code on a bracelet or lanyard. Watch what happens when a stranger—a Good Samaritan—finds our patient, Ravi, wandering in a park and scans his bracelet.”

*Action: Hold up your phone and scan the demo QR code. Show the phone screen.*

### 1. Zero-Friction Access
“The moment they scan, a secure, ultra-lightweight web app opens instantly. **No app downloads, no logins, no sign-ups.** Time is critical, and we remove every barrier.”

### 2. Immediate Empathy & Context
“The finder isn't just given a phone number. They are immediately given the patient’s name, their condition, and crucial calming instructions. They know exactly how to speak to Ravi to avoid triggering a panic episode.”

### 3. Medical AI Assistant
“If Ravi is showing physical distress—maybe he is shivering, or clutching his chest—the Good Samaritan doesn't have to guess. They can chat directly with our specialized **Lyzr Medical AI Agent**. This AI has secure, read-only access to Ravi’s medical profile.
If the finder types, *'He is sweating and looks dizzy,'* the AI analyzes his chart and might reply: *'Ravi has Type-2 Diabetes and is on Glipizide. He is likely experiencing hypoglycemia. Please offer him a sugary drink immediately and call his emergency contact.'* It turns a helpless stranger into an informed first-responder.”

### 4. Live Geo-Location Matching
“Finally, the stranger can open the Map tab. We use real-time Leaflet integration to show the stranger's exact GPS location plotted against Ravi’s registered home address. The finder can visualize exactly how far Ravi has wandered and can hit a single button to share their live coordinates directly with Ravi's Guardian.”

## 🏆 The Impact
“The Good Samaritan Scanner takes the burden of an Alzheimer's crisis off the shoulders of a confused patient, and empowers everyday people to actively save a life using just their smartphone camera and our AI architecture.”
